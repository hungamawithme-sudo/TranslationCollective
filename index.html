About the Collective

Translation Collective is a literary translation collective working between German and major Indian languages. We focus exclusively on the translation of novels, short stories, essays, and literary non-fiction, approaching each text with close attention to style, voice, and cultural context.

Our collective model brings together translators with deep linguistic and literary expertise, enabling collaborative, critically informed translations from German into Indian languages and vice versa, as well as through English as a bridge language where required.
