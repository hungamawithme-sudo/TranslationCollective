About Us

Translation Collective is a literary translation collective dedicated to culturally sensitive and stylistically precise translations between German and major Indian languages. We focus on the translation of novels, short stories, essays, and other literary texts, working from German into Indian languages and vice versa, as well as through English as a bridging language. Our collective brings together translators with deep literary, cultural, and theoretical awareness, ensuring that each translation preserves the aesthetic, historical, and linguistic integrity of the original text

Our Services

German → Indian Languages

Literary translation of German novels, short stories, essays, and literary non-fiction into major Indian languages, with close attention to style, voice, and cultural context.

Indian Languages → German

Translation of literary works from Indian languages into German, preserving narrative rhythm, cultural specificity, and aesthetic depth.

German ↔ Indian Languages (via English)

Indirect literary translation using English as a bridge language, especially for multilingual and comparative literary projects.

Literary Editing & Revision

Stylistic editing, comparative revision, and consistency checks for translated literary texts, manuscripts, and publications.

  • English ↔ Malayalam
  • German ↔ Malayalam
